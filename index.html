After finishing high school, I made a conscious decision to challenge myself and grow beyond what I knew. That decision led me to enroll at the Taiwan-Paraguay Polytechnic University, where my journey in Industrial Engineering began.

This mindset later brought me to Taiwan, where I spent several semesters at NTUST. Living in a different culture challenged me to adapt quickly, communicate effectively, and develop a broader international perspective.

Today, I'm focused on building a professional career where I can connect across cultures, contribute meaningfully, and keep growing in a global environment.
